Frequently Asked Questions

What's the minimum order quantity?

For standard models (55" floor, 32" desktop), MOQ is 1 unit. For custom OEM/ODM, MOQ is 10 units.

What's included in the price?

Complete hardware (kiosk + screen), pre-installed software (digital human, AI, voice), 2-year warranty, technical documentation, and remote installation support.

How long does delivery take?

Standard models: 15-20 days. Custom OEM: 30-45 days. International shipping adds 7-14 days depending on location.

Do you provide installation support?

Yes! We provide detailed installation guides, video tutorials, and remote technical support. On-site installation available for large orders (10+ units).

Absolutely! You can customize appearance (clothing, hair, accessories), voice, personality, language, and gestures to match your brand.
